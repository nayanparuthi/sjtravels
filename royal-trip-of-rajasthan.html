Program ( 12 Days )

  • Day 01
    1

    Arrival at Delhi – Jaipur

    On youR arrival at the Delhi airport, you will be met by our representatives. They will receive you and escort you to Jaipur, the capital of Rajasthan. After reaching there, check in at the hotel and stay overnight.

  • Day 02
    2

    Jaipur Sightseeing

    he day is reserved for the full-day sightseeing tour of Jaipur. In the first half, you will explore Amber Fort, Jagmandir or Hall of Victory, Jal Mahal, Lake Pichola and Goddess Kali Temple. In the latter half, the highlights are City Palace, Hawa Mahal and Jantar Mantar. Dinner and overnight stay will be at the hotel.

  • Day 03
    3

    Jaipur - Kota – Bundi

    In the morning, you will leave Jaipur and proceed towards Kota. After reaching there, you will explore the Taragarh Fort, the Palace and the 84-pillared cenotaph. Thereafter, you will move for Bundi and check in at the hotel, on arrival. Overnight stay will be at the hotel.

  • Day 04
    4

    Bundi - Udaipur

    You will leave Bundi for Udaipur, in the morning. The arrival will be followed by a check in at the hotel. Thereafter, you will leave for visiting City Palace Museum and Jagdish Temple. Overnight stay will be at the hotel.

  • Day 05
    5

    Udaipur Sightseeing

    OSightseeing tour of Udaipur is planned for today, with City Sahelion Ki Bari, a cruise in Lake Pichola and a visit to Jag Mandir Palace as its highlights. In the evening, you can go for shopping in the local market of Udaipur. Overnight stay will be at the hotel.

  • Day 06
    6

    Udaipur – Jodhpur

    A seven-hour drive will start in the morning and take you to Jodhpur. Enroute, you will visit Ranakpur Jain Temples, belonging to 15th century. After reaching there, you will check in at the hotel and spend the rest of the day at leisure. Overnight stay will be in the hotel.

  • Day 07
    7

    Jodhpur – Jaisalmer

    Early in the morning, you will visit Mehrangarh Fort, Umaid Bhawan Palace, Jaswant Thada, and Clock Tower. By afternoon, you will leave for Jaisalmer, visiting 7th century Ossian Jain temple enroute. After reaching there, you will check in at the hotel and retire for the night.

  • Day 08
    8

    Jaisalmer Sightseeing

    After breakfast, you will embark on a full day sightseeing tour of Jaisalmer and visit Jaisalmer Fort, Jaisalmer Havelis, Jain Temples, Sam Sand Dunes and Gadsisar Sagar Lake. Overnight stay will be in the hotel.

  • Day 09
    9

    Jaisalmer – Bikaner

    On this day, you will drive to Bikaner and check in at hotel, on arrival. Thereafter, a visit to Junagadh Fort, Bhand Sagar Temple, Lal Garh Palace and Camel Breeding Farm will be on the cards. Overnight stay will be in the hotel.

  • Day 10
    10

    Bikaner – Mandawa

    This day will see you leaving Bikaner and proceeding towards Mandawa. After reaching there, you will check in at the hotel and freshen up. Thereafter, you will visit the magnificent havelis of the place. Overnight stay will be in the hotel.

  • Day 11
    11

    Mandawa – Delhi

    In the morning, you will drive down to Delhi and check in at hotel, on arrival. After lunch, you will go on a sightseeing tour of Old Delhi and visit Red Fort, Jama Masjid, and buzzing streets of Chandni Chowk. Overnight stay will be at the hotel.

  • Day 12
    12

    Departure from Delhi

    EIn the morning, you will leave for visiting New Delhi and drive past British-era monuments, such as President's Palace, Parliament House and India Gate. Thereafter, you will explore Akshardham Mandir Humayun's Tomb, Iskcon Temple and Qutab Minar. By late evening, you will pack your bags and leave for the airport, from where you will catch the flight to your destination.
