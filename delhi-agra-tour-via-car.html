Program ( 1 Day )

  • 1

    Delhi to Agra By Car

    n early morning after breakfast you will be picked up to Delhi and proceed for Agra by road. The Mughal City Agra is most famous for the Taj Mahal.

  • 2

    Visit Sikandara Tomb

    Around 10 km from Agra, lies Sikandra, there is a big mausoleum of Emperor Akbar. This structure is more unique in Agra. It is borrowing from Hindu, Christian, Islamic, Buddhist and even Jain architectural conventions.

  • 3

    Visit Taj Mahal And Lunch

    The Taj Mahal, as we all know about the most famous place in India Taj Mahal.There is a lot sights to watch for all visitors to India. It is count as the seven wonders of the world. It is the symbol of great love.It was built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in loving memory of his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal who died while giving birth to their fourteenth child. The building is built by pure white marble It is located in the banks of Yamuna River. The Taj spreads across almost 50 acres land and it is believed that this particular spot was chosen by the emperor that's why it is peaceful place and soothing effect. Love and all who visit at there the first impression created by the marvel in white marble. After visiting Taj Mahal you will be go for lunch.

  • 4

    Lunch at Restaurant

    After visiting Taj Mahal you will be go for lunch.

  • 5

    Visit Agra Fort

    In afternoon you will visit Agra Fort constructed by Emperor Akabar in 1565 A.D and includes many buildings like the Moti Masjid and Musanman Burj, Where Shah Jahan is believed that he has been imprisoned and died.

  • 6

    Drive back to Delhi

    Finally you will proceed back to Delhi along with an impression of TaJ and a sheer magic that the Taj exudes making it an unforgettable and cherished image forever.

  • 7

    Arrive Delhi pick up & drop to your Hotel/Airport, Railway Station.

    On arrival at Delhi you will be dropped at your hotel or House for your journey.
