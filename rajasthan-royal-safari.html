Program ( 10 Days )

  • Day 01
    1

    Arrive Delhi

    rrive at Indra Gandhi International Airport, New Delhi. Upon Arrival you are welcomed and assisted by the local representative after that transfer to the hotel and your rest of day is free for leisure and overnight at the hotel.

  • Day 02
    2

    Delhi

    Breakfast at the hotel and we start our full day tour with Old Delhi, First of all we visit Red fort after that we visit Jama Masjid and we take a rikshaw ride as well. After that we visit India gate, Raj ghat etc. In the evening we visit Kutub minar and later we come back to the hotel and overnight at the hotel.

  • Day 03
    3

    Augue invidunt has

    reakfast at the hotel and today we drive to Agra and enroute we visit Sikandara and we continue our drive to Agra and check in at the hotel and later we see the most beautiful building in the world. The Taj Mahal and after we that we visit Agra fort and we come back to the hotel and Overnight at the Hotel.

  • Day 04
    4

    Agra – Jaipur (233 Km / 5hrs)

    Breakfast at the hotel and today we drive to Jaipur and enroute we visit Fatehpur – Sikri (Once capital of King Akbar) after that we continue our journey to Pink City, Jaipur. Upon arrival we check in at the hotel and our day is free for leisure and overnight at the hotel.

  • Day 05
    5

    Jaipur

    Breakfast at the hotel and today we visit Jaipur. We start with Amber fort there we reach amber fort by Elephant ride and after seeing Amber fort weVisit to City Palace & Museum and later on we visit Hawa Mahal. (Palace of winds). In the evening we come back to the hotel and overnight at the hotel.

  • Day 06
    6

    Jaipur – Pushkar

    Breakfast at the hotel and later we drive to Pushkar and Check in at the hotel and today we visit Brahma Temple and Pushkar lake, Overnight at the hotel.

  • Day 07
    7

    Pushkar – Udaipur

    Breakfast at the hotel and today we drive to Udaipur, Check in at the hotel and our rest of day is free for leisure and overnight at the hotel.

  • Day 08
    8

    Udaipur

    Breakfast at the hotel and today we visit some famous places in Udaipur Such as City Palace & Museum, Jag Mandir , Jagdish Temple , Sheliyon ki bari & in the afternoon we do boat ride at Lake pichola & later we come back to the hotel and Overnight at the hotel.

  • Day 09
    9

    Udaipur - New Delhi

    Breakfast at the hotel and later timely departure to Airport to take flight to New Delhi. Upon Arrival in New Delhi and we are transferred to the hotel and check in at the hotel .Full day free for leisure and Overnight at the hotel>

  • Day 10
    10

    New Delhi – Departure

    Breakfast in the morning and timely departure to Indira Gandhi International Airport to take home bound flight.
