Program ( 16 Days )

    Upon arrival in Delhi, India, you will be received by Grand Tours India Representatives with the warmest of welcomes at the airport / railway station from where you would be escorted to the pre booked hotel. Freshen up at hotel. Dinner and overnight stay will be at hotel.

  • Day 02
    2

    Delhi

    Today, after hearty morning breakfast at hotel, the Rajasthan Village Tour package takes you on a visit to the attractions of Delhi, India. See the Red Fort, Jama Masjid and Raj Ghat drive past the Parliament House and India Gate and tour the Laxmi Narayan Temple, Lotus Temple and other Delhi attractions. Come back to the hotel for dinner and overnight stay.

  • Day 03
    3

    Mandwa

    The Rajasthan Village Tour package takes you to Mandawa in Rajasthan on day 03 of the tour after a hearty breakfast at hotel. Upon arrival in Jaipur, Grand Tours India Representatives will assist you to transfer to the hotel. Tour the Saraf Haveli, Ladia Haveli and the Chokhani Haveli. Dinner and overnight stay will be at hotel./p>

  • Day 04
    4

    Mandwa

    Have hearty breakfast at the hotel and then drive to the tourist attractions of Lacchmangarh, Nawalgarh, Fatehpur and Jhunjhunu as a part of the Rajasthan Village Tour itinerary. Come back to the hotel in Mandawa for dinner and an overnight stay.

  • Day 05
    5

    Bikaner

    Have a hearty morning breakfast at hotel. Upon arrival in Bikaner, Grand Tours India Representatives will assist you to transfer to the hotel. Relax a while at hotel and embark on tours to the Camel Breeding Farm, Junagarh Fort and other local attractions located in and around Bikaner. Come back to the hotel for dinner and overnight stay.

  • Day 06
    6

    Khimsar

    Have hearty morning breakfast at hotel and then drive to Khimsar as a part of the Rajasthan Village Tour. Upon arrival in Khimsar, Grand Tours India Representatives will assist you to transfer to the hotel. Freshen up and relax at hotel. Dinner and overnight stay will be at hotel.

  • Day 07
    7

    Khimsar

    After a relaxed breakfast in the morning, today the Rajasthan Village Tour takes you on a jeep safari to the Bishnoi settlements located in and around Khimsar. Come back to the Khimsar Fort heritage hotel for dinner and an overnight stay..

  • Day 08
    8

    Nagaur

    Have a hearty morning breakfast and then drive to Nagaur with the Rajasthan Village Tour. On arrival at Nagaur, Grand tours India Representatives will assist you to transfer to the hotel. Get the feel of Rajasthan splendor as you relax in plush tents replete with all modern amenities. See the Nagaur Fort and other tourist attractions of Nagaur before retiring to the hotel for dinner and an overnight stay.

  • Day 09
    9

    Kuchaman

    After hearty breakfast in the morning, drive to Kuchaman on day 09 of the Rajasthan Village Tour. Upon arrival at Kuchaman, Grand Tours India Representatives will assist you to transfer to the hotel. Relax during the rest of the day. Dinner and overnight stay will be at hotel..

  • Day 10
    10

    Kuchaman

    Have a hearty morning breakfast and then drive to the Sambhar Lake and enjoy an interesting bird watching tour. Enjoy a packed lunch and drive back to Kuchaman for an overnight stay.

  • Day 11
    11

    Roopangarh

    Have hearty breakfast in the morning and then drive to Roopangarh as a part of Rajasthan Village Tour package. Upon arrival, Grand Tours India Representatives will assist you to transfer to the Roopangarh Fort heritage hotel. Tour the attractions of Tilonia Village and Kishangarh and see local craftsmen creating splendid works of art. After sightseeing, come back to the hotel for dinner and overnight stay.

  • Day 12
    12

    Pushkar

    Have a hearty breakfast in the morning and then drive to Pushkar. Upon arrival at Pushkar, Grand Tours India Representatives will assist you to transfer to the hotel. Set out on guided tours to the holy Pushkar Ghats, tour the Brahma Temple and Savitri Temple. Dinner and overnight stay will be at hotel.

  • Day 13
    13

    Samode

    After hearty breakfast in the morning, drive to Samode. Upon arrival, Grand Tours India Representatives will assist you to transfer to the hotel. Relax and set out on tours to the places of tourist interest located in Samode. Stay overnight at the heritage hotel in Samode.

  • Day 14
    14

    Samode

    Have a hearty breakfast. Today, You are free to spend the day, as you like. Stay overnight at the hotel in Samode, Rajasthan, India.

  • Day 15
    15

    Neemrana

    Have a hearty breakfast in the morning and then drive to Neemrana as a part of the Rajasthan Village Tour. Upon arrival, Grand Tours India Representatives will assist you to transfer to the hotel.

  • Day 14
    16

    Delhi - Tour ends

    The Rajasthan Village Tour comes to an end as you drive back to Delhi and continue your journey back home.
