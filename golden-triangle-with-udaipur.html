Program ( 8 Days )

  • Day 01
    1

    Arive Delhi

    On arrival you will be transferred to your hotel. Overnight: New Delhi

  • Day 02
    2

    Delhi

    The first Morning after breakfast proceed for a half day city tour, you will be escorted to see The India Gate, The Parliament House, The President House, Qutab Minar, and Humayun's Tomb. Our representatives will also guide you through the ticketing process as well as the history and importance associated with the sites. Afternoon, drive to Agra. On arrival check into hotel in Agra. Overnight : The first Morning after breakfast proceed for a half day city tour, you will be escorted to see The India Gate, The Parliament House, The President House, Qutab Minar, and Humayun's Tomb. Our representatives will also guide you through the ticketing process as well as the history and importance associated with the sites. Afternoon, drive to Agra. On arrival check into hotel in Agra.

  • Day 03
    3

    Agra ( By road )

    Visit the world famous Taj Mahal at dawn, see the sun rising over the Taj Mahal and the marble changing shades with the sunlight falling on it., after breakfast we proceed to the Agra fort famous for it's beautiful palaces, and the Jasmine Tower where the Mughal emperor Shahjahan was confined to as a prisoner for the last 8 years of his life. After Luch we see Itmad-ud-daulah also famous as the baby taj and is said to be the precursor of the Taj Mahal. Evening free for independent activities, you can utilize this time for shopping the famous Agra marble handicrafts as well as a lot of rugs and carpets that are available in abundance. Overnight: Agra

  • Day 04
    4

    Agra - Jaipur ( By road ) enroute visiting Fatehpur Sikri

    Start you drive to jaipur (230 Kms), see fatehpursikri enroute, the deserted capital of the Mughal emperor Akbar. On arrival check in to your hotel and after lunch start for a half day city tour of Jaipur We visit the Hawa Mahal and the Birla temple Evening - a walking tour of the colorful streets and bazaars of Jaipur City. Our representatives attending to you can guide you to the best destinations to but gemstone jewelery, pots as well as great traditional outfits. Overnight: Jaipur

  • Day 05
    5

    Jaipur

    Take a full day long sightseeing tour of Jaipur city that includes the Jantar Mantar observatory, See the city palace, which is one of the main attractions of Jaipur. Built by the Kachhwaha ruler of Amber, Maharaja Jai Singh II in the early 18th century, the City Palace is an impeccable blend of the Rajasthani and Mughal forms of architecture. The huge palace complex which houses some fine structures, courtyards, beautiful gardens and buildings, occupies one seventh of the pink city. A lot of additions inside the palace complex were made by members of the royal family during subsequent years. Visit the Amber fort, you'll ascend till the gates of fort on elephant back. Fort displays a beautiful mix of Mughal and Rajput styles of architecture. We will ensure that you get adequate help to see these great structures and you can get easy access to area that would interest you. Dinner at Choki Dani - where you may enjoy traditional Rajasthani dinner. Overnight: Jaipur

  • Day 06
    6

    Jaipur - Udaipur ( By road )

    In the morning Drive to Udaipur on arrival check in at your hotel. Rest of completely on your discretion, you also have the option of exploring the bazaars (markets) for some good miniature paintings and handicrafts. In evening take a sightseeing tour of the breathtaking royal buildings around the Lake in boat tour. Overnight: Udaipur.

  • Day 07
    7

    Udaipur

    Take a full day sightseeing trip of the city which includes visit to the City Palace Udaipur's fascinating City Palace is the largest royal complex in Rajasthan. Standing on a rocky promontory the palace has balconies, towers and cupolas and presents a wonderful view of the lake and the city. The complex has eleven constituent mahals (palaces), constructed by successive maharanas during the three hundred years that followed the foundation of Udaipur in 1559. Saheliyon ki bari - The "garden of the maids of honour", Sahelion-ki-Bari was designed and laid out early in the eighteenth century as a retreat for ladies of the royal household to spend their time in leisure and excursion to temples of Nagada and Eklingji. In the evening you visit the Jagdish temple – Built in 1651 A.D. by Maharana Jagat Singh, this Indo-Aryan temple is dedicated to Lord Jagannath, a manifestation of Vishnu. The largest and the most beautiful temple of Udaipur, it is always seething with constant activity and noted for its beautiful sculpted images and towering shikhara. Overnight: Udaipur

  • Day 08
    8

    Delhi - Departure

    In the morning you will be transferred to the airport to board the flight for New Delhi. You will be received at New Delhi airport by our representative and half day tour of New Delhi –Visit the Red Fort, built by Mughal Emperor Shahjehan, Jama Masjid, one of the biggest Mosque of India, enjoy the rickshaw ride, visit the Raj Ghat, the cremation sites of Mahatama Gandhi. Also drive past Chandni Chowk, a famous Delhi area. Transfer to the airport for onward journey.
